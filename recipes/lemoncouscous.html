Lemon Couscous!

Description

Couscous is an interesting dish, similar to the consistency/style of rice. It starts out very hard, but when mixed with water and butter, it swells up and gets nice and fluffy. It can be great on its own with salt and pepper, but really shines when other things are mixed into it.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Couscous
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Sea salt
  • Garlic
  • Lemon zest
  • Lemon juice
  • Minced parsley
  • Pine nuts

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring the water to a boil. Add the couscous, olive oil, and sea salt and stir. Cover, remove from heat, and let stand for 5 minutes.
  2. Fluff the couscous with a fork, transfer it to a serving bowl, and toss is with more olive oil, sea salt, garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, parsley, and pine nuts.
