Guacamole!

Description

Guacamole is a delicious dip for chips and other snack foods, primarily made up of mashed avocados. In ours, we prefer to add mango and tomatoes to give it a richer flavor. It is superior to salsa in pretty much every way.

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Mangos
  • Tomatoes
  • Jalapeno Peppers
  • Limes
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Dice the mango, tomato, and jalapeno.
  2. Juice the lime.
  3. Mash the avocado in a large bowl. Add the mango, tomato, onion, lime juice, and jalapeno, stirring until combined.
  4. Add cilantro and salt last.
