Grape Salad!

Description

Grape salad, basically, is the greatest dish to ever grace the table. everything about it is perfectly delicious, and everybody who has ever eaten it has said that it's amazing. There's not too much of a reason to dislike it. It tastes fresh, it's moderately healthy, and it has all sorts of fun, tropical flavors in it.

Ingredients

  • grapes
  • pineapple tidbits
  • a whole lotta mayo
  • orzo
  • chicken
  • white pepper
  • celery
  • half and half
  • salt
  • granulated sugar
  • lemon juice
  • cucumber

Steps

  1. Cut the grapes in half and chop the cucumber and celery.
  2. Cook the chicken breasts to 165 degrees and cube them.
  3. Cook orzo to desired doneness.
  4. Drain and rinse the orzo in cold water.
  5. In a large bowl, combine mayo, half and half, salt, sugar, lemon juice, and white pepper.
  6. Stir in cooked orzo and remaining ingredients.
  7. Mix well, cover, and refrigerate.
